TACTICAL NON-NEGOTIABLES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Goalkeeper must act as eleventh outfield player in possession phases.
- Centre-backs split wide; full-backs invert or push high depending on phase.
- Midfielders must offer passing angles between the lines.

Pressing System:
- High press triggered on backward passes or poor first touches in wide areas.
- Forwards initiate press; midfielders cut passing lanes.
- Goalkeeper sweeper role essential when playing high line.

Defensive Line:
- Average height: 42–48 metres from own goal in possession.
- Offside trap coordination required from all back four plus goalkeeper.
